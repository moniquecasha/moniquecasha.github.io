Project Description

A logo design for the 2018 Salesian Summer Camps at Savio College, Ħad Dingli. Inspired from the 2018 Strenna by the Rector Major dealing with "two elements of vital importance in today's world: listening and personal accompaniment." This design was printed on t-shirts of three different colours, application forms, lyric sheets etc.
