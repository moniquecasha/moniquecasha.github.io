MONIQUE CASHA

I am a highly creative visual artist with a passion for graphic design and photography. Inspired by all things creative, I am seeking to represent myself, my vision of the world as well as that of people around me through my work. Through multiple aspects of my life I have had the opportunity to greatly channel and expand my creativity, which have led to the development of a steady background within the field of digital arts, mainly visual design and creative approach. Currently, I am reading for my Bachelors of Fine Arts in Digital Arts at the University of Malta, which is to be completed later on in 2019.
